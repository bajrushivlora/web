Conscious App

UX / UI
Mobile App Design
Wireframing

This is the Conscious app. This app is targeted to people who are new to living a low-waste lifestyle, are looking for a beginner-level app about sustainability, and are interested in trying to live a sustainable lifestyle that is specific to their lifestyle. I know that many people are deteered from such a lifestyle because it’s seen as an all-or-nothing deal. Either you are completely zero waste, or you’re labeled as a trash-producing, careless human.

I think we need to revise this way of thinking.

The app will give users a chance to try and live as sustainably as possible according to their lifestyle. People have responsibilites, and not a lot of us can dedicate time and effort to living a 100% zero waste life, and that’s okay. The point of the app is that you should do what you can to be as eco-friendly as possible and it focuses on positive reinforcement rather than shaming people for producing trash, hence why you can write down what sustainable habits you did on any particular day.

My end goal for the user is to be motivated by the decline in trash that they are producing, if they are recording their trash often. They will see an autopsy of their trash contents, and hopefully see what unnecessary trash they are producing. With this, the user should also see an increase in sustainable habits that they’ve been integrating in their daily report to the app.
